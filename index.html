Maya the Busy Flower Shop Owner

Small Business Owner managing orders, deliveries, and customer payments daily

Maya owns and operates a local flower shop where she manages everything from customer orders to deliveries and invoicing. She handles payments manually while juggling a busy schedule, which often leaves her feeling overwhelmed and disorganized.

Goals

  • Keep all invoices and payments organized in one place.
  • Quickly see which customers have paid and which invoices are overdue.
  • Automate reminders and tracking so she can focus on running her business.

Frustrations

  • Tracking payments across different places is confusing.
  • Forgetting invoices or follow-ups when business gets busy.
  • Too much manual tracking and guesswork.
